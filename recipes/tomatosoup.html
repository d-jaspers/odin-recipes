Tomato soup

Description

This is a recipe for the creamiest tomato soup you've ever had and it only takes a bit over an hour to cook.

By following this recipe, you'll have enough tomato soup for 8 servings, so you can have a nice family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed chicken broth, undiluted
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped onion
  • Â¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. In a large stock pot, combine tomatoes, chicken broth, butter, sugar, onion, and baking soda. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat; reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 hour.
  2. Stir in cream; cook until heated through and serve.
